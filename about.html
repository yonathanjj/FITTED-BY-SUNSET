MEET BETSELOT_

I am Betselot — a self-taught maker from Ethiopia, crafting clothing that blends comfort, style, and soul. Every piece is made by hand, from cutting the first thread to the final stitch. I believe clothing should feel good to wear, last for years, and carry a story you’re proud to share.

My process is slow and intentional. I choose fabrics that are soft yet durable, inspired by earthy tones and timeless silhouettes. I make each garment with the hope that it becomes a cherished part of your everyday life.
